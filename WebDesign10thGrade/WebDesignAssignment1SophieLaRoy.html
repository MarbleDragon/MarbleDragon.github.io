<h1>About the Play</h1>

<h2>Vampires</h2>

<p>The <cite>Children of the Night</cite> is an original theatre production written and brought to life by a local homeschool drama group. It follows a group of kids trying to save their town as a <b>mysterious coalition</b> led by Mr. Ike Wonky spreads a <b>vampire infection</b> through town.

<blockquote>A man who truly knows the youth of today, a man who is not afraid to face the facts, a man who prides himself on traditional family values. You may want to put on sunglasses as we welcome to the stage, a ray of positivity, <strong>Mr. Ike Wonky!</strong>

<br/>

 -Mr. Ike Wonky</blockquote>

</p>

<h2>Time Period</h2>

<p>The story takes place in the <b>80s</b>, and includes a variety of callbacks and references for all the <b>80s kids</b> out there, while still being fun and engaging for a range of ages. One main theme of the play is the <b>Satanic Panic</b> of the 80s, and there’s also a variety of styles and items that were popular in the 80s.</p>

<h1>How to Watch It</h1>

<h2>Where</h2>

<p>The <cite>Children of the Night</cite> Theatre production will take place at the <i>Strand Theatre</i> in <i>Pontiac.</i></p>

<h2>When</h2>

<p>Opening night is on the <b>8th of May,</b> 2025. But if you miss opening night, it is also being performed on May <b>9th</b> and <b>10th</b>, 2025.</p>
